Education Support

At Mother Care Charity, we believe education is the foundation of a brighter future. Our programs are designed to break the cycle of poverty by providing quality education, scholarships, and skill development opportunities for children and youth.

Our Initiatives

  • Providing school supplies, uniforms, and books to underprivileged children.
  • Offering scholarships for higher studies.
  • Running free evening classes and tutoring programs.
  • Organizing skill development workshops for youth.
