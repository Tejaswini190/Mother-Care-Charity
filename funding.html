What We Fund

We direct funds to programs with measurable impact and full transparency.

Education & School Support

Scholarships, school supplies, after-school classes and community learning centers for children.

Healthcare & Medical Camps

Mobile clinics, medicines, maternal health programs and health awareness drives.

Livelihood & Skill Training

Vocational training, micro-grants and job placement support for youth and women.

How Donations Are Used

60% Program Costs: Direct services — education, health, food and shelters.

20% Community Projects: Training, livelihoods and local partnerships.

10% Emergency Relief: Rapid response for disasters and urgent medical needs.

10% Admin & Fundraising: Operations, monitoring and transparent reporting.

We publish quarterly reports and receipts for every funded project. For detailed reports, email info@mothercare.org.
