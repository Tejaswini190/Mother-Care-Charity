Disaster Relief

Natural disasters leave thousands homeless and vulnerable. Mother Care Charity responds with immediate relief and long-term recovery plans to help communities rebuild.Disasters is a broad term they are sudden events that cause significant damage, disruption, or loss of life. They can be natural, human-made, or a mix of both.

Emergency Aid

We distribute food, water, medicine, and temporary shelter to families affected by floods, earthquakes, and other disasters. Man-made disasters include industrial accidents, fires, chemical leaks, and conflicts.

Community Recovery

Charities play a crucial role in helping people and communities when disasters strike. When floods, earthquakes, fires, or other crises occur, many organizations step in to provide immediate relief such as food, clean water, medical care, and safe shelter. Once the emergency phase has passed, they also help with long-term recovery by rebuilding homes and schools, supporting health systems, and offering counseling for survivors who may be coping with trauma

Medical Support

Our teams provide first-aid, emergency medical care, and essential medicines to injured and sick individuals during crises.Floods are the most frequent natural disaster, accounting for 40% of all disasters.Medical support provides urgent care for injuries, prevents disease outbreaks, and ensures people continue to receive essential treatments during and after a crisis.

Psychological Counseling

We offer counseling services to trauma-affected individuals, helping them cope with stress, fear, and emotional loss.Earthquakes cause the highest number of deaths per disaster event.Psychological counselling helps disaster survivors cope with stress, grief, and trauma so they can recover emotionally and rebuild their sense of safety.
