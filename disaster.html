Disaster Relief

Natural disasters leave thousands homeless and vulnerable. Mother Care Charity responds with immediate relief and long-term recovery plans to help communities rebuild.

Emergency Aid

We distribute food, water, medicine, and temporary shelter to families affected by floods, earthquakes, and other disasters. Man-made disasters include industrial accidents, fires, chemical leaks, and conflicts.

Community Recovery

Beyond immediate relief, we help rebuild homes, schools, and healthcare centers to restore normalcy in disaster-hit areas.Disasters cause loss of lives, displacement, destruction of homes, and economic losses.

Medical Support

Our teams provide first-aid, emergency medical care, and essential medicines to injured and sick individuals during crises.Floods are the most frequent natural disaster, accounting for 40% of all disasters.

Psychological Counseling

We offer counseling services to trauma-affected individuals, helping them cope with stress, fear, and emotional loss.Earthquakes cause the highest number of deaths per disaster event
