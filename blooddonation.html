Blood Donation

Every drop counts. Blood donation is one of the most selfless acts that can save lives. At Mother Care Charity, we organize regular blood donation camps to help patients in need of immediate transfusions. One unit of blood can save up to three lives.

Our Blood Donation Camps

We collaborate with hospitals and health organizations to host safe and hygienic blood donation camps. Volunteers are guided by professional medical staff to ensure the process is smooth and secure.

How You Can Help

✔ Register as a blood donor with us
✔ Volunteer at our blood camps
✔ Spread awareness about safe and regular blood donation
Together, we can ensure that no life is lost due to blood shortages.
