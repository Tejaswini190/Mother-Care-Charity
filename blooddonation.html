Blood Donation

Every drop counts. Blood donation is one of the most selfless acts that can save lives. At Mother Care Charity, we organize regular blood donation camps to help patients in need of immediate transfusions. One unit of blood can save up to three lives.

Our Blood Donation Camps

✔ Organized in collaboration with hospitals
✔ Professional medical staff supervision
✔ Safe and hygienic donation environment
✔ Smooth and secure process for every donor

How You Can Help

✔ Register as a blood donor with us
✔ Volunteer at our donation camps
✔ Spread awareness about regular blood donation
✔ Encourage friends & family to participate

Emergency Support

✔ Rapid response for urgent needs

Impact of Blood Donation

✔ One donation saves up to 3 lives
