Clothing & Shelter

A safe place and proper clothing are basic human rights. At Mother Care Charity, we provide warm clothes, blankets, and temporary shelters to families in need.

Clothing Drives

  • Collecting clothes from donors across communities.
  • Distributing warm clothes during winter seasons.
  • Providing footwear and daily wear essentials.
  • Reaching remote and rural areas in need.

Shelter Support

  • Temporary housing for homeless individuals.
  • Safe spaces for families during emergencies.
  • Providing beds, blankets, and hygiene kits.
  • Ensuring dignity and protection in crisis.

Winter Relief Programs

  • Distribution of woolen clothes and blankets.
  • Special focus on children and elderly people.
  • Organizing camps in flood and cold-hit regions.
  • Reducing seasonal illnesses through care.

Long-term Shelter Projects

  • Building community shelters in rural areas.
  • Creating safe housing for orphaned children.
  • Collaborating with local bodies for support.
  • Ensuring sustainable living conditions.
